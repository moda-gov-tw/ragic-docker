Top 10 signs that you have outgrown your spreadsheets

1. You've got over 30K rows of data.
2. You're using vlookups and sumifs.
3. You have no single version of the truth.
4. You have multiple authors for a single spreadsheet.
5. You're trying to make your data not editable or readable by some people.
6. Spreadsheets take too long to maintain and limit your ability to provide accurate, timely reports.
7. You cannot meet compliance and audit requirements.
8. You realize your data is not clean, is in multiple formats, and contain errors.
9. When your boss asks for some data fast, and you know you can't get it.
10. You grow despondent when your company announces any changes that will affect your spreadsheets.
