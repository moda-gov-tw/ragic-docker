Ragic new feature — auto cross sheet edits Hi *|FNAME|*,

Ragic wants to make everyone an awesome database developer and we mean it. One of our most frequently requested feature is to create and update records across sheets automatically. And now we present: custom action buttons that can auto update records on another sheet.





These action buttons that you can set and forget help you to automate part of your workflow.

Creating an action button can be done through your Form Tools menu within the Design Mode. Here are three types of actions you can set up:

Convert Records

Your users will be able to click an action button in your source sheet to create a copy of your data (which can be all of your fields, or a selection of your field values) to be saved in another sheet.
Whether it's to create a quote or invoice from a sales order, or to post contact information to a team roster, this method makes data entry more efficient for your team.



Update Values

Sometimes you might want to update field values on a record without manually inputting the values, especially for repetitive actions in your workflow. For this, you can create an update values action button that will run an update for your record without entering the editing mode.



Update Values on Another Sheet

Great for inventry management and posting numerical values, you can use this action button to increase or decrease a numerical value on another sheet with this action button. Examples include issuing goods from a Sales order to change the inventory of the products, or running balances of an account according to your transaction records.
